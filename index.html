Philadelphia is located in the state of Pennsylvania. It’s one of the oldest cities in the United States. It is the place where both the constitution and the declaration of Independence were signed in the year 1787.

Why did I choose Philly? well, it’s because I always wanted to travel there. They have 35 square blocks of old buildings from the 18th and 19th centuries. There are multiple art and culture museums around the area as well that I am itching to see. And of course, the main reason I want to visit is to try their Philly cheesesteaks.
